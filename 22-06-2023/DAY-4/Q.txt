****************Circular Q******************
*In normal queue concept even if there is space after
 deleting items we would not be able to utilize those 
 spaces in order to use of spaces we are coming to 
 the concept called circular queue
1. Initialising Circular Queue (front=rear=-1)
2. Insertion happens at rear & Del happends at front
3. Inserting first element (front=rear=0)
4. Insertion from second element (rear=rear+1)& Insert|
5. After deletion front element (front=front+1)
